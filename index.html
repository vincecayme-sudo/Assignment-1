What is a Digital Twin?

A digital twin is a virtual model that replicates the physical characteristics, behavior, and performance of an actual object or system.

It allows users to simulate, monitor, and analyze the physical asset in a virtual environment.

Digital Twins: A closer look

Digital twins are used across various industries including manufacturing, healthcare, and smart cities. They help in predictive maintenance and operational efficiency.
